4.1 Limitations de Vitesse Permanantes - Permanent Speed Reduction Boards

TIV fixe à distance (du type «B») - Fixed speed warning board (> 140km/h) 
     +---+       blk/wht/blk semicircle board
     |nnn|
       -         reduce to nnn km/h by the next TIVR, Km, or Z board
       |         only for trains authorised to travel at or above 140 km/h
       |         this will be the top board in a stack of TIVs
TIV fixe à distance (du type «C») - Fixed speed warning board (railcar) 
       -         blk/wht/blk semicircle board
     |nnn|
     +---+       reduce to nnn km/h by the next TIVR, Km, or Z board
       |         only for railcars and self-propelled passenger units
       |         this will be below type B but above ordinary in a TIV stack

     +---+       blk/wht/blk square board
     |nnn|
     +---+       reduce to nnn km/h by the next TIVR, Km, Z board or
       |            interlocking
       |         may also be repeated at incoming lines

       .

4.11 Manoeuvres: Signaux a Main et Sonores - Hand/Whistle Switching Signals

Tirez - Pull 
__ __           2 long blasts
                up/down movement of rolled flag or white lamp
Refoulez - Push 
__ __ __        3 long blasts 
                horizontal swing of rolled flag or white lamp
Ralentissez - Slow 
__ _ __ _ __ _  3 (long + short) blast pairs
                short vertical movement of rolled flag at arm's length or
                   white lamp next to body
Arrêtez - Stop 
_ _ _ _ _ _     several short blasts
                waving of red flag, display of red lamp, or
                   violent movement of hands, any flag or lamp
Lancez - Shove to release after decoupling 
__ __ _         2 long + 1 short blast
                rolled flag or white lamp moved twice away from body
                   and quickly down
Appuyez - Press to couple/decouple (note European coupling) 
                flat hands pressed together one or more times
                   (one with lamp at night)
