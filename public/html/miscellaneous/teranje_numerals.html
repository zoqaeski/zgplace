Teranė Numerals

This page contains the multiplication and division tables for the Teranė, or bijective decimal, numeral system. Bijective decimal is almost, but not quite, decimal; instead of having zero, a placeholder for ten is used. Thus 30 (in base 10) is written as 2ȣ (twenty ten).
