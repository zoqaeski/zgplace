Qevesa Grammar

Syntax

Qevesa is a head-final language, so dependent elements usually precede their heads.

Word Order

The basic word order in Qevesa is tp np np... pv etc. This is a highly flexible word order, although cannot be described as a free word order because the phrases have a fairly rigid internal word order.

Sostita zanoimaš litásan tó-latismuvir.

sostit-a woman-nom
zanoim-aš potter-acc
litás-an bread-dct
tó=latis-m-u-vir. aff=cook.dyn;ipfv-3sg.erg-decl-ewts

As for the woman, she is baking bread for the potter.

Zanoima sostitam litásan tó-latisvášuvir.

zanoim-a potter-nom
sostit-am woman-erg
litás-an bread-dct
tó=latis-váš-u-vir. aff=cook.dyn;ipfv-3sg.acc-decl-ewts

As for the potter, the woman is baking bread for him.

Litása zanoimaš sostitam tó-latisňonuvir.

Litás-a bread-nom
zanoim-aš potter-acc
sostit-am woman-erg
tó=latis-ňon-u-vir. aff=cook.dyn;ipfv-3sg.dct-decl-ewts

[It is] bread that the woman is baking for the potter.
