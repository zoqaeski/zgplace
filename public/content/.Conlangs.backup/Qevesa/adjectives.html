Qevesa Grammar

Adjectival Morphology

Inflection

Adjectives must agree in case with the nouns they modify. They are normally placed before the noun, however may be placed after the noun to place special emphasis on it; note that in this case any postpositions follow the adjective, not the noun. Adjective declension is fairly straightforward, and similar to the declension of nouns, with one big difference: adjectives always end in -i.

Adverbs

Adverbs are created by replacing the word-final -i with -iro.

Comparatives and Superlatives

The comparative and superlative forms are created by adding the suffixes -jož- (comparative) and -moq- (superlative) to the stem of the adjective. Likewise, dimunitive forms are created by adding -kiri to the stem.
