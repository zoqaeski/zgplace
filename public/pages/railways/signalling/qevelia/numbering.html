Qevelian Railway Signalling

Track and Signal Numbering

Tracks, signals and other items of infrastructure are numbered in the Down direction, that is, away from the start point of the line. Normally this is the (largest) station closest to the coastline by rail; unlike on some other railways, every line is reset at the station from which it diverges. This is primarily due to the non-radial (i.e. pluricentric) nature of the Qevelian network.
