Qevelian Railway Signalling

Signals on Vehicles

There are a number of different signals and signs displayed on moving and stationary vehicles.

Signals on Train Movements

Headlights

The leading end of a train movement is indicated by three white lights arranged in a triangle with the peak facing up.

Tail lights

The rear end of a train movement is indicated by one or two steady or flashing red lights.

Other lights

The rear end of locomotives is indicated by a single white light. Note that for multiple-unit consists, the white light is only shown on the rear locomotive. Assisting locomotives with a separate driver display headlights and a rear light.

Signals on Shunting Movements

Shunting movements are indicated by two horizontal white lights on the leading end.
