Qevesa Grammar

Pronomial Syntax

Personal Pronouns

Qevesa is a pro-drop language, as subject and object pronouns of the first and second person are usually omitted before the verb; by not doing so, the speaker gives the subject special emphasis. The same may also be done for third person, but this occurs much less frequently.

Reflexive and Reciprocal

The reflexive pronoun vyň is used for reflexive constructions within a single clause:

Vyňeš vekósa ikíšhëtëran.
