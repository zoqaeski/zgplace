Qevesa Grammar

Adjectival Morphology

Qevesa distinguishes between attributive and predicative adjectives. The former follow the pattern CeCaCi and do not decline to case or agree with the noun, although they can be modified with the polarity proclitics. Predicative adjectives are essentially modified forms of the verb.

The attributive adjectives can be turned into adverbs by replacing the word final -i with -a and then casting it in the instrumental case. Like the attributive adjectives, adverbs can also be modified with the polarity proclitics.

  • Adjective: pelati beautiful
  • Adverb: tó-pelatátti beauty, with beauty
  • Negative adverb: mó-pelatátti without beauty

Adjectival Declension

Predicative adjectives decline in a similar manner to verbs, although most conjugation involves the use of suffixes rather than transfixes. Although only aspectual marking is shown here, a predicative adjective can be marked with all of the categories that are applicable to a verb.

Comparison

The Comparative Degree

The comparative degree is formed by placing the proclitic opóš before the adjective or adverb. The standard of comparison is formed by placing it in the comparative case.

A-a B-eňöc t’opóš-dylvéňonuvir.

A-a A-nom
B-eňöc. B-cmp
t=opóš=dylvé-ňon-u-vir aff=more=big.stv;ipfv-3;inanim.dct.top-decl-ewts

“A” is bigger than “B”.

The Superlative Degree

The superlative degree is formed by the circumfix o—eš, which is placed around the adjective or adverb.

A-a t’odylvéňonešuvir.

A-a A-nom
t=o>dylvé-ňon<eš-u-vir. aff=<most>big.stv;ipfv-3;inanim.dct.top-decl-ewts

“A” is the biggest.

The Dimunitive Degree

A dimunitive degree also exists, although is used far less often. It consists of the proclitic mëš or as the circumfix mi—eš. The proclitic is used for the dimunitive comparative, and the circumfix for the dimunitive superlative.

A-a B-eňöc tómëš-dylvéňonuvir.

A-a A-nom
B-eňöc. B-cmp
tó=mëš=dylvé-ňon-u-vir aff=less=big.stv;ipfv-3;inanim.dct.top-decl-ewts

“A” is not as big as “B”.

A-a tómidylvéňonešuvir.

A-a A-nom
tó=mi>dylvé-ňon<eš-u-vir aff=<least>big.stv;ipfv-3;inanim.dct.top-decl-ewts

“A” is the least biggest.
