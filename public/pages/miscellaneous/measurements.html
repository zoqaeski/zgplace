Time

The following units are used for time:

Unit Symbol Definition Equivalent Relation to SI
viukit vk 1 second = 0.8544 seconds
mirat mr 72 vk minute = 1.02528 minutes
qevra qr 72 mr hour ≈1:13:49.21 hours
jältü jt 24 qr Teranë day = 29.528064 hours, or ≈29:31:41.03 hours

A Teranë year is 297 days long, or ≈365.4 terrestrial days long.
