Duodecimal / Decimal Converter Calculator

This is a quick converter to switch between decimal and duodecimal. Decimal field uses the digits 0–9; duodecimal field uses 0–9 and T for 10, E for 11. Both use a period as a decimal marker. The precision is truncated to the input.
