Qevelia Travel Guide

Getting In

The Qevelian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Immigration and Customs operates passport and border control. A visa and passport are required for all international visitors except those from neighbouring countries. Note that passports function differently on Terrania, and you require it to be stamped when you arrive and when you leave a country; they are divided into two parts for this purpose. Visas can be applied for at both the Qevelian Embassy in your country, and at the customs office of your point of arrival, however note that in the latter case a small fee applies.

There are strict customs controls on goods imports, not as an inhibitor to trade but to prevent pests and environmental problems from getting in. You will more than likely be required to have your baggage screened for these using x-ray scanners, and the only things that may be blocked are items of biological origin, including foodstuffs and plant and animal matter. Failure to hand over prohibited goods will result in a major fine; see the section on laws and regulations below.

Asides from this, getting in is relatively easy.
